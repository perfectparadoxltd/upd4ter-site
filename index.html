What it does

F.01
[↓]

One-click updates, verified

Evergreen downloads the update, checks the code signature and verifies the developer's Team ID matches the app you already trust — before a single byte is replaced. If anything fails, automatic rollback.

F.02
[◎]

Sees what the App Store can't

Most of your apps came from the web, not the App Store — and nothing keeps them updated. Evergreen tracks them via Homebrew's catalog, Sparkle feeds and the open Evergreen database.

F.03
[▴]

Lives in your menu bar

Checks quietly in the background on your schedule. One leaf icon, a badge when something's new, a notification only when it matters. Close the window — Evergreen keeps watch.

F.04
[⇣]

Download Only mode

Don't want any app touching your /Applications? Evergreen can download and signature-verify the update, then drop it in your Downloads folder. You install it yourself.

F.05
[¶]

Release notes built in

See what changed before you update — release notes pulled straight from the developer's feed, side by side with the version you're on.

F.06
[x]

Private by design

Your app inventory never leaves your Mac. No accounts, no analytics, no phoning home. The update database is public — you can read every line of it on GitHub.
