The Short Version

Evergreen runs entirely on your Mac. Your files, content, and activity inside the app never leave your computer. We have no servers that receive your data, no analytics SDK, no tracking.

The only personal data we (or our payment partner, Paddle) handle is what is needed to sell you a licence and answer your support emails. That's the whole story — the rest of this document spells it out.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data in connection with Evergreen (the "App"), our website https://perfectparadox.co.uk, and licence sales.

This policy is issued in compliance with:

  • The UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • The Data Protection Act 2018
  • The Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR)
  • The EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR), where applicable to EU-based users

For the limited personal data described in Section 3, Perfect Paradox Ltd is the data controller. For your purchase transaction, Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record and is an independent data controller for the payment data it collects (see Section 4).

For everything you do inside the App, there is no controller and no processor — because the data never leaves your device and we never receive it.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

This is the most important section of this policy. The App does not collect, access, transmit, store, or process:

  • Your files or documents — anything you open, create, or edit with the App stays on your Mac.
  • Your content or activity — what you do in the App is not logged, measured, or reported to anyone.
  • Usage analytics or telemetry — the App contains no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no crash-reporting service that sends data automatically, and no phone-home functionality beyond what is disclosed in Section 5.
  • Personal identifiers from your Mac — no advertising IDs, no fingerprinting, no contact lists, no location.

This is verifiable: you can monitor the App's network activity with any firewall tool (e.g., Little Snitch or LuLu) and confirm that the only connections it makes are the ones listed in Section 5.

4. Payments: Paddle as Merchant of Record

We sell Evergreen through Paddle (Paddle.com Market Ltd, UK / Paddle Payments Ltd for certain regions). Paddle is the Merchant of Record: when you buy a licence, your purchase contract for the transaction is with Paddle, which resells the App on our behalf and handles payment processing, VAT/sales tax, invoicing, and refunds.

What this means for your data:

  • Paddle collects your name, email address, billing address/country, and payment details (card number, etc.) directly at checkout. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Paddle shares with us your email address, country, and order details so we can deliver your licence key and provide support.
  • Paddle is an independent data controller for the data it collects. Its handling of your data is governed by Paddle's privacy policy: https://www.paddle.com/legal/privacy

5. The Only Network Connections the App Makes

The App makes only the following network connections, and nothing else:

(1) connections to GitHub, the Homebrew formulae API, and developer update feeds to check for and download app updates (no user data is sent); (2) Sparkle update check for Evergreen itself against perfectparadox.co.uk; (3) licence activation/validation against Paddle's licensing API

  • Update check (Sparkle): the App periodically asks our update server whether a newer version exists. This request transmits the App version, your macOS version, and basic CPU type — no personal data, no unique identifiers used for tracking. You can disable automatic update checks in the App's preferences.
  • Licence activation/validation (Paddle): when you enter your licence key, the App contacts Paddle's licensing API to verify it. This transmits your licence key and an activation identifier for your Mac. It happens at activation and occasionally thereafter to confirm the licence is valid.

If the App is offline, it keeps working — none of these connections are required for normal day-to-day use after activation.

9. Data Security

For the small amount of personal data we hold: data in transit is encrypted (TLS 1.2+), stored data is encrypted at rest, and access is limited to authorised personnel. For everything inside the App, security is inherited from your own Mac — your data is exactly as secure as your machine, because it never goes anywhere else. We recommend FileVault and regular backups.

10. Children
